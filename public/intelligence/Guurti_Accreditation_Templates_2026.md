# Guurti Diplomatic Accreditation Toolkit (2026 Standard)

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Executive Summary**
> This toolkit has been reverse-engineered from the successful 2026 IMF / World Bank Group Spring Meetings accreditation process for Hon. Abdikadir Hassen and Mahmoud Awaleh. It establishes the official Guurti standard operating procedure (SOP) and email templates for securing high-level parliamentary accreditation to global forums (UN, EU, SIPRI, Paris Peace Forum).

---

## Template 1: Initial Parliamentary Accreditation Request
*To be sent from `protocol@guurtigovsomaliland.org` or `pgeu@guurtigovsomaliland.org` to the event's Parliamentary/Government Relations team.*

**Subject:** Official Accreditation of the Parliamentary Delegation – Republic of Somaliland

**Body:**
Dear Secretariat / Parliamentary Relations Team,

On behalf of the Committee on Foreign Affairs, Planning, International Cooperation & Investment of the House of Elders (Guurti), Republic of Somaliland, I am pleased to submit the official accreditation request for our parliamentary delegation to attend the upcoming [Name of Event, e.g., 2026 Spring Meetings / Stockholm Forum].

Please find below the details of the Head of Delegation:
*   **Name:** Hon. [Full Name]
*   **Title:** Chairman, Foreign Affairs Committee
*   **Institution:** House of Elders (Guurti), Parliament of the Republic of Somaliland
*   **Official Email:** [e.g., chair.fac@guurtigovsomaliland.org]

If a dedicated registration portal or specific accreditation link is required for Parliamentarians, please provide the link so we may complete the individual registrations for each member of our delegation.

We look forward to participating in the ancillary meetings and contributing to the global dialogue.

Kind regards,

**[Your Name / Protocol Officer]**
Parliamentary Relations Team
House of Elders (Guurti), Republic of Somaliland
[Contact Number]

## Template 2: Status Inquiry (Post-15 Day Review Period)
*Based on the IMF/WB standard 15-business-day review period. To be sent if no confirmation is received.*

**Subject:** URGENT: Status of Parliamentary Accreditation – Hon. [Name]

**Body:**
Dear Registration Services,

We received your automated acknowledgment on [Date of automated email] regarding the parliamentary accreditation application for Hon. [Name] (Application Email: [Email Address]). 

As the standard 15-business-day review period governing Parliamentarian accreditation has now concluded, we are requesting a formal update on the status of this registration. 

Please advise if any additional documentation or official government credential verification is required from the Secretariat to finalize the confirmation letter, as this documentation is urgently required for our delegation’s travel planning and visa processing.

Sincerely,

**Parliamentary Relations Team**
House of Elders (Guurti), Republic of Somaliland

## Template 3: Official Visa Support Letter (Note Verbale)
*To be generated internally once the Confirmation Letter/Badge is received (e.g., the April 03 IMF/WB Confirmation). To be submitted to the relevant Embassy.*

**Subject:** Note Verbale – Request for Diplomatic/Official Visa

The Secretariat of the House of Elders (Guurti) of the Republic of Somaliland presents its compliments to the [Name of Embassy, e.g., Embassy of the United States / Embassy of Sweden] in [Location, e.g., Nairobi / Djibouti].

The Secretariat has the honor to request the issuance of an official visa for Hon. [Full Name], who has been formally registered and accredited to attend the [Name of Event, e.g., 2026 Spring Meetings of the IMF and the World Bank Group] taking place in [City, Country] from [Start Date] to [End Date].

Attached to this Note Verbale is the official Confirmation Letter from the [Event Organizer, e.g., World Bank Group Registration Team], which is required for the issuance of the event badge. 

The Secretariat of the House of Elders avails itself of this opportunity to renew to the [Name of Embassy] the assurances of its highest consideration.

**[Date & Official Guurti Seal]**

## Template 4: Internal Delegate Travel Briefing
*To be sent internally to the MP once registration is successful.*

**Subject:** CONFIRMED: Travel Protocol for [Event Name]

Dear Hon. [Name],

We are pleased to inform you that your parliamentary accreditation for the [Event Name] in [City] is fully confirmed. 

**Mandatory Travel Requirements:**
1. **Confirmation Letter:** You MUST print the attached official Confirmation Letter and travel with a hard copy. This letter is non-transferable and is required to pick up your official badge at Registration.
2. **Identification:** You must bring your valid government-issued photo ID (Diplomatic Passport).
3. **Email Access:** Please ensure you have active access to your official email (`[your.email]@guurtigovsomaliland.org`) on your mobile device, as the organizers may send ancillary meeting updates.

Please confirm receipt of this briefing.

Best regards,
**Protocol Office**
