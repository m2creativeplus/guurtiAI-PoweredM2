# GUURTI Strategic Calendar: Global International Events (June – August 2026)

This curated list of international events for **Summer 2026** is specifically aligned with the strategic mandate of the **Golaha Guurtida (House of Elders of the Republic of Somaliland)**. 

The events are categorized to support the Guurti's primary pillars: **Parliamentary Diplomacy**, **Peacebuilding & Conflict Resolution**, and **African Regional Integration**.

---

## 📅 June 2026

### 1. 52nd Ordinary Session of the AU Permanent Representatives' Committee (PRC)
- **Date:** June 3–17, 2026
- **Location:** AU Headquarters / Virtual
- **Guurti Relevance:** High. While Somaliland is not a recognized AU member state, the EPD can leverage "corridor diplomacy" with friendly nations' representatives during this two-week session to distribute briefings on Somaliland's democratic stability.

### 2. World Bank Fragility Forum 2026
- **Date:** June 8–10, 2026
- **Location:** Washington D.C. / Hybrid
- **Guurti Relevance:** **Critical.** The Guurti is the architect of Somaliland's peacebuilding model. The EPD should submit a side-event proposal or whitepaper demonstrating how indigenous conflict resolution (the Guurti model) out-performs top-down international state-building.

### 3. IPU Regional Seminar: Human Rights in the Digital Era
- **Date:** June 8–10, 2026
- **Location:** Warsaw, Poland (Hosted by the Sejm and Senate of Poland)
- **Guurti Relevance:** Medium. Opportunity to engage with European parliamentarians (Twelve Plus Group) on issues of digital sovereignty and electoral integrity, contrasting Somaliland's biometric voting systems with regional instability.

### 4. 4th International Conference on Environmental Peacebuilding
- **Date:** June 16–19, 2026
- **Location:** TBD
- **Guurti Relevance:** High. Climate change and pastoralist land disputes are critical Horn of Africa issues. The Guurti's traditional mechanisms for resolving grazing/water conflicts are highly relevant here.

### 5. 8th Mid-Year Coordination Meeting of the AU and RECs
- **Date:** June 27, 2026
- **Location:** TBD (Africa)
- **Guurti Relevance:** High. Involves Regional Economic Communities like IGAD. The Guurti EPD should monitor IGAD policy shifts and prepare independent "Somaliland Stability Assessments" to share with IGAD delegations.

### 6. International Day of Parliamentarianism
- **Date:** June 30, 2026
- **Location:** Global
- **Guurti Relevance:** **Actionable.** The EPD should launch a digital diplomacy campaign on this day, highlighting the Guurti's 35-year history as Africa's most successful hybrid bicameral parliament.

## 📅 July 2026

### 7. International Conference on Peacebuilding and Humanitarian Support
- **Date:** July 7, 2026
- **Location:** Ottawa, Canada
- **Guurti Relevance:** High. Canada is a key target for Somaliland parliamentary diplomacy. Sending a Guurti delegation to present on "State-building without International Recognition" could yield significant bilateral engagement.

### 8. Parliamentary Forum at the UN High-Level Political Forum (HLPF)
- **Date:** July 14, 2026
- **Location:** UN Headquarters, New York
- **Guurti Relevance:** **Critical.** Focuses on SDG 11 (sustainable cities and communities). The Guurti can highlight Hargeisa's rapid, peaceful urbanization compared to other Horn of Africa capitals. Requires coordination with the Somaliland Representative Office in the US.

### 9. Joint 9th Session of the AU Specialized Technical Committees (STCs)
- **Date:** July 20–24, 2026
- **Location:** AU Headquarters
- **Guurti Relevance:** Medium. Focuses on Finance, Trade, and Integration. Important for monitoring the AfCFTA (African Continental Free Trade Area) implications for the Berbera Port corridor.

### 10. 17th Workshop of Parliamentary Scholars and Parliamentarians
- **Date:** July 25–26, 2026
- **Location:** Wroxton College, Oxfordshire, UK
- **Guurti Relevance:** **High.** Co-sponsored by the Inter-Parliamentary Union (IPU). The UK is a historical partner to Somaliland. A Guurti academic delegation should present a peer-reviewed paper on Somaliland's upper house architecture.

- **Guurti Relevance:** Medium-High. Further cements Somaliland's narrative as a beacon of peace and leadership in an otherwise volatile Horn of Africa/Red Sea region.
